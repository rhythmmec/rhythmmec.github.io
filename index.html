Sports Impact Monitor

The Sport Impact Monitor is a universalized device meant to measure the impact force and pressure force of target objects. Looking at the picture above, we see the product is composed of an ADXL 345 accelerometer, breadboard, raspberry pi 2, jumper wires, and an independent power supply. Sports Impact Monitor's features include calculating acceleration, impact force, email notification using twilio. The Sports Impact Monitor also returns a graph of the calculated acceleration and impact force using python without needing the user to go through the specific coordinates. Said features vary based on the dependent or independent mode of the Sports Impact Monitor.
